You can select one of the following topics for your internship but not limited to:
# Topic Details
1 Evacuation planning in emergency situations In a volcano eruption or tsunami, an early warning for evacuation will help to save many lives. However, it is challenging to broadcast exact messages to
hundreds of thousands of people to evacuate in a way:
(1) preventing the situation of many people going in a road and congestion occurring so slowing down the evacuation;
(2) broadcasting messages based on the user's location.
(3) Create a clear evacuation map for people to follow
Keywords: Google evacuation map, https://artsandculture.google.com/asset/the-evacuation-route-map-office-of-the-prime-minister/RQGRlNc0T68HZQ?hl=en
2 Signal surveillance in restricted-access areas To detect suspicious and bad guys in a restricted-access area without risking their life of direct physical intrusion, law enforcement
may need a tool to monitor whether there are human activities in a specific building, detect the exit/entrance doors in the building or walkable areas. In this research, you will
explore to use signal localization techniques to (1) draw the building shape from signal traces (2) predict the movement directions of the targets inside.

Keywords: Signal tracking, Object sensing
3 Specific brand IoT device detection In important networks, reliable devices come from trusted sources are a must to maintain the integrity of network traffic. However, due to cost,
many citizens may not fully follow the regulation. They may still use several old devices or come from unreliable sources. This task is to detect
IoT devices (smart speaker, smart lamp, smart bridge...) that come from specific countries or unreliable sources

Keywords: IoT device detection, IoT device identification

Refer example#: https://github.com/kahramankostas/IoTDevIDv2
4 Ransomware/Malicious attack detection Currently, ransomware is a severe threat to many critical applications and network infrastructure. In this topic, we will explore
new deep learning models to detect ransomware/malicious attacks in public dataset. Besides, the ultimate goal is to explore the ransomware network or
injected chain (use MITRE ATT&CK)

Keywords: Intrusion detection system, DDoS attack detection, Ransomware detection

1. Ransomware dataset: https://github.com/manabu-hirano/RanSAP
2. IoT dataset https://www.stratosphereips.org/datasets-iot23 or https://www.unb.ca/cic/datasets/iotdataset-2023.html
5 Deep fake/AI-generated content detection Currently, deep fake is one of the biggest threats to civil applications or politic campaign. New AI technologies can enhance even further the
authenticity of information. Detection content/images/videos generated by AI or deep fake videos is then a critical matter. In this topic, you will explore
several techniques to detect the deep fake/AI-generate videos/images, e.g., by face swap, mouth swap

Keywords: Disinformation, deep fake detection, AI-generated content detection

Dataset: https://github.com/Daisy-Zhang/Awesome-Deepfakes
6 Integrated sensing and communications (ISAC) ISAC is one of three pillars for 6G. However, this technology is still at the early state of development. In this topic, you will explore
methods to using ISAC to model the problem of detecting UAVs, smart phone's location in the environment without Internet

Keywords: cellular sensing, device-to-device sensing

Refer: https://github.com/PolyuWirelessCommunicationLab/Data-Association-in-Network-Device-Free-Sensing
